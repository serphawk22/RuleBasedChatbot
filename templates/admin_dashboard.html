Admin Access & Collaboration

To allow other people to manage the chatbot rules:

  1. Open the Google Sheet.
  2. Click the Share button (top right).
  3. Add their email (e.g., collaborator@gmail.com) and set them as an Editor.
  4. They will then be able to update rules which the bot will automatically fetch.

Note: To give them access to this dashboard, share the login password (allybot1122) with them.
